  • Total Runs: Over 3,000
  • Centuries: 1
  • Half-Centuries: 28
  • Highest Score: 122* (not out)
  • Fastest to 1,000 Runs: Achieved in the fewest innings
  • Most Runs in a T20 World Cup: Leading run-scorer in multiple editions

IPL HIGHLIGHTS

Virat Kohli: IPL Legend A look into his remarkable IPL career with RCB

Virat Kohli has been a stalwart of the Indian Premier League (IPL), leading the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) for many years. His explosive batting and leadership have made him one of the most prominent figures in the tournament. Here are some highlights of his IPL career:

  • Matches Played: Over 200 IPL matches
  • Total Runs: Over 6,500
  • Centuries: 5
  • Half-Centuries: 44
  • Highest Score: 122* (not out)
  • Most Runs in a Single Season: 973 runs in 2016
  • Most Runs in a Calendar Year: Holds the record for the most runs in a single calendar year (2016)
  • Captaincy: Longest-serving captain of RCB

Notable Performances:

  • 2016 IPL Season: Scored 973 runs in 16 matches, leading RCB to the final and winning the Orange Cap
  • 2009 IPL Season: Played a key role in RCB’s run to the final
  • 2011 IPL Season: Consistent performances throughout the season, helping RCB in crucial matches

TEST HIGHLIGHTS

Virat Kohli: Test Series Maestro A glimpse into his extraordinary Test career

Virat Kohli has been a formidable force in Test cricket, showcasing his batting prowess and leadership qualities across various series. Here are some highlights of his Test career:

  • Matches Played: Over 110 Test matches
  • Total Runs: Over 8,000
  • Centuries: 27
  • Highest Score: 254* (not out)
  • Fastest to 1,000 Runs: Achieved in the fewest innings
  • Most Runs in a Calendar Year: Holds the record for the most runs in a single calendar year (2016)
  • ICC Test Player of the Year: Multiple-time winner
  • Captaincy Record: Successful captain with numerous series wins

Notable Performances:

  • 2018 Tour of Australia: Scored 684 runs in four Tests, including two centuries in a single Test match in Perth
  • 2016 Test Series Against West Indies: Scored 365 runs in four matches, including two centuries in the series
  • 2017 Test Series Against Sri Lanka: Scored 610 runs in three Tests, including a double century and a triple century in the series
